How I Think

01local_fire_department

You don't manage a backlog. You manage belief.

Conviction is contagious — so is doubt.
01local_fire_department
If the team doesn't believe in what they're building, no process will save you. Your job is to make the mission undeniable — then get out of the way.
02search

PRD before discovery? Not a spec — it's a story you made up.

Discovery buries the wrong ideas. Fast.
02search
Most PRDs are written to justify a decision already made. Real discovery means being willing to kill your favorite idea before lunch.
03speed

Velocity without clarity is expensive motion.

Speed amplifies direction — good or bad.
03speed
Shipping fast means nothing if you're headed nowhere sharp. The best teams aren't fast — they're focused. Clarity is the multiplier.
04edit_note

Narrative is a data compression device.

Words are the new code. Use them precisely.
04edit_note
A great one-pager beats a 40-slide deck. Storytelling is like fortune-telling — frame the future clearly enough and people will build it.
05balance

Capacity doesn't stretch. It trades.

Every yes is a silent no.
05balance
The most important skill in product leadership isn't prioritization — it's the discipline to say no clearly. When everything is a priority, nothing ships well.
06help

Great PMs make the right questions unavoidable.

The right question outweighs a dozen solutions.
06help
You're not the answer key. You're the pressure that reveals truth. Ask questions so clear that the answer becomes obvious.
